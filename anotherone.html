About FreshCatch Seafood

Your trusted source for premium seafood since 2015.

For over a decade, we've built direct relationships with fishermen from Maine to Alaska, ensuring you get the freshest catch possible. We specialize in sustainable, responsibly sourced seafood delivered to your door or available for pickup.

Our team of seafood experts hand-selects each item, guaranteeing quality you can taste. From wild-caught salmon to farm-raised oysters, we bring the ocean's best to your table.

  • Next-day delivery in the tri-state area
  • Same-day pickup available
  • Sustainable & traceable sourcing
  • Expert customer service
